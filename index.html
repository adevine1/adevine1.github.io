Get Five!

This is a clone of a popular 5-dice game (i.e., Yahtzee) that I wrote in HTML/CSS/JS/JQuery. It is based a hand-held device that I've played to death over the years. I cloned it a number of years ago on Windows, and I wanted to recreate the experience as closely as I could on the web. It uses local storage to persist high-score data between sessions.
